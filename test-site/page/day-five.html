Three Rules to Follow

  1. Choose Right Image Format:
    • JPEG: For photographs
    • PNG/GIF: For illustrations or pictures with flat colors(solid)

  2. Choose Right Size: The dimension of the image you put in the folder should be the same to the dimension of the image on the website. This will ensure the image will show properly and load the image faster.

  3. Choose the Right Resolution: For JPEG and PNG, the resolution should be 72 ppi max. GIF's resolution is different. It relies on the GIF dimension.
